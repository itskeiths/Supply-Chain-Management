SUSTAINABLE SUPPLY CHAIN DASHBOARD

Our Sustainable Vendor and Supplier Tracking Dashboard is a cutting-edge solution tailored for businesses committed to sustainability and operational excellence. This project aims to provide a comprehensive view of your supply chain, from vendor management to product delivery, while emphasizing eco-friendly practices and cost-effective strategies.

Dataset

Description

The dataset collected from a Fashion and Beauty startup focuses on the supply chain of Makeup products. It includes information such as product type, SKU, price, availability, number of products sold, revenue generated, customer demographics, stock levels, lead times, order quantities, shipping details, supplier information, and production metrics. This comprehensive dataset provides insights into product performance, customer behavior, and various supply chain parameters, enabling a thorough analysis for sustainable vendor and supplier tracking.
